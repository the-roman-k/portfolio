Taxation Platform

Comprehensive tax management system designed for enterprise clients, featuring automated calculations, compliance tracking, and intuitive reporting interfaces.

The Challenge

The Taxation Platform was a legacy Windows-only desktop product for large corporate clients. It was outdated, complex, and burdened with serious usability issues. There were no clear processes, roles, understanding and clear expectations of how should it be and how end result looks like.

The goal

The primary goal was to transform an outdated, Windows-only taxation platform into a modern, cloud-based web solution — introducing a new brand identity, updated look and feel, and delivering a more efficient, user-friendly experience for corporate clients.

The secondary goal was to build a Design System and design environment to support product evolution.

The tertiary goal was to establish clear processes for distributed teams to work together efficiently.

How I got there

I started by reviewing all inputs: documentation, video instructions, and demo sessions. To clarify goals and validate expectations, I conducted stakeholder interviews, mapped the current "as-is" state, and defined a "to-be" vision with an implementation roadmap.

I set up the design environment and created onboarding guidelines for new team members. I built functionality maps, sitemaps, role-based maps, scenario maps, and other flows to capture the existing experience and explore future ones.

For the UI, I introduced a new brand identity and developed a complete model covering spacing, scrolling, navigation, interactions, layouts, components, and patterns. Several complex flows were delivered as sub-products to meet customization needs.

I also documented the design system and its usage, and facilitated regular workshops to align distributed teams, set clear expectations, and improve collaboration.

Product Mapping

I created a full product mindmap to outline its structure, nested modules, and relationships. Starting from the "as-is" state, I transformed it into a "to-be" vision based on the available inputs at that time.

Roles Mapping

I created detailed role-based maps to capture how different user roles interact with the product. Each role required its own perspective, modules, and entities — resulting in tailored interfaces and unique user experiences.

Deliverables:
  • Role-based mindmaps

Customer Journey Map

I designed a set of customer journeys for each role, mapping end-to-end experiences to highlight unique needs, pain points, and opportunities across the product lifecycle.

Deliverables:
  • CJMs (Customer journey maps)

Sitemap

I created a global sitemap to show the shared interface across all roles, and developed dedicated sitemaps for each role to capture their unique modules and pages not available to others.

Deliverables:
  • All roles Sitemap

Roadmaps

After analyzing the full structure, I collaborated with the team to create detailed roadmaps. These visualized timelines, responsibilities, modules, and milestones — helping us plan execution, sequence deliveries, and align on priorities.

Roadmaps were constantly reviewed as many changes appeared and more and more features come from Client side.

Every major Module had own Roadmap as dedicated teams were assigned.

Deliverables:
  • Global Roadmap
  • Roadmaps per Module

Grid System

As a starting point, I defined the main UI layouts to manage the product's complex nature. I also designed a comprehensive responsive behavior model to ensure the interface adapts seamlessly across different screen sizes.

Deliverables:
  • Grids

Spacing Model

I developed a spacing model for the complex interface to handle numerous nested components. The goal was to preserve clear visual hierarchy and relationships, ensuring readability and intuitive grouping for the end user.

Deliverables:
  • Spacing guidelines and tokens

Nav Model

I designed a complex navigation model to address role-specific views, modules, and nesting levels within the interface. Because each role required a different structure, I developed tailored navigation models that preserved clarity across multiple levels while ensuring a smart, scalable solution.

Deliverables:
  • Navigation models

Team Alignment Workshop

In the early stages, I noticed many uncertainties and misalignments within the team. To address this, I organized a team alignment workshop where we clarified the project vision, shared pain points and expectations, and brainstormed solutions.

Together we prioritized actions and defined clear outcomes for each member — building trust, reducing stress, and enabling more confident, efficient collaboration.

After this workshop, the amount of friction and hard decisions was drastically reduced, and every team member gained a clear backlog, along with a solid understanding of their responsibilities and what to expect from others.

Deliverables:
  • Workshop framework
  • Outcomes for every team member

Design Process

After initiating design activities and conducting the alignment workshop, I established a clear design process for the team. This process explained how to collaborate with designers, how the project lifecycle works, and how each step is executed in sequence.

I also created role-specific task descriptions for business analysts, designers, and other contributors to ensure all vital inputs were delivered. These efforts helped move the team from delivery chaos to structured, controlled execution.

Deliverables:
  • Design process guidelines
  • Team tasks mapping

Design System

A dedicated design system was established to ensure consistency of experience and UI components across the product and its modules. It provided a unified foundation for layouts, interactions, and visual patterns, enabling scalable growth and alignment across teams.

Deliverables:
  • Design system

Since designers and contributors joined from different teams with varying levels of experience, the onboarding included clear guidance on working with existing mock-ups and the design system. It also explained the anatomy of design files, delivery files, testing files, and other assets used in the product — ensuring faster adaptation and smoother collaboration.

Deliverables:

  • Design Onboarding Package
